# NYXO Heading Weight Adjustment

## Decision

Keep Alumni Sans Variable for the landing page display headings and reduce only the primary `h1` and section `h2` weight from `900` to `700`.

## Constraints

- Do not change the typeface, font sizes, line heights, tracking, layout, or copy.
- Do not change the NYXO wordmark, product names, process numbers, or interface typography.
- Verify the computed heading weight and responsive overflow after the change.
